Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

Ramadan - Fasting & Prayer Times ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Location Data: We use your device's location solely to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Location data is processed on your device and is not sent to our servers.

App Data: Your fasting records, Quran progress, goals, and settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays ads to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase data through Apple's App Store. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Aladhan API: Provides prayer time calculations. Only your approximate location (latitude/longitude) is sent to retrieve prayer times. Aladhan Privacy Policy

Data Storage

All personal data (fasting logs, Quran progress, settings, reflections) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time through the App's Settings > Reset Data option. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
